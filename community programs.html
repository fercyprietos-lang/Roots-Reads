Community Programs

Explore cultural and educational activities designed to inspire curiosity and connection.

📚 Reading Local Filipino Literacy

Children explore Filipino storybooks written by local authors. After reading, they join our Story Circle Sharing to express their insights about the stories and connect culture with personal experiences.

🌐 Community Forum

A space for adults to share insights, reflections, and engage in open discussions about Filipino literature, heritage, and community experiences.
