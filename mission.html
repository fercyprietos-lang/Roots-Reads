Our Mission

Roots & Reads aims to preserve and promote Filipino culture by making local stories and heritage accessible to everyone. We believe that stories connect generations and strengthen cultural identity.

Through digital platforms, community programs, and educational resources, we strive to ensure that Filipino traditions, folktales, and literature continue to inspire future generations.
